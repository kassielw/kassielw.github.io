about me

Hi! I'm Kassie Wang, a rising sophomore at Cornell University, intending to major in computer science and possibly double major in statistics as well. Ever since being exposed to statistics in high school, I have dreamed of combining my passion for coding with it in the field of data science, especially given our era of big data. On campus, I am a part of the Insights subteam of the Cornell Data Science project team. Furthermore, I always believe in helping others whenever I can, leading to my interest in tech for social good as well.

Fueled by my passion to help others and curiosity of the world, I am always seeking new opportunities to leverage my knowledge of computer science. I'm also always open to meeting new friends. So, please feel free to check out my resume below or just reach out!

Outside of work, I am an urban/hip-hop dancer and am grateful to be a part of Cornell's BreakFree Hip-Hop dance team. Feel free to check out some of my videos here. If I'm not at the library with friends or the dance studio, then you most definitely will find me exploring the food nearby.
